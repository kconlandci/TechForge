Data Deletion Instructions

TechForge: IT Training

How to Delete Your Data

All your data (lab progress, streaks, XP, and usage statistics) is stored locally on your device. TechForge does not store any personal data on external servers.

To delete all your data:

  1. Open TechForge on your device.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Reset Progress.
  4. Confirm the reset when prompted.

This will permanently delete all lab progress, scores, streaks, and XP from your device.

Uninstalling the App

Uninstalling TechForge from your device will also remove all locally stored data, including progress and analytics.

Firebase Anonymous Account

TechForge creates an anonymous Firebase account (no email or personal data attached). This account is automatically cleaned up by Firebase after a period of inactivity. No action is required on your part.
