Privacy Policy

TechForge: IT Training
Effective date: April 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

TechForge uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to generate a random user ID (UID). We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Information

The anonymous UID is used solely to associate your in-app purchase entitlements (via RevenueCat) with your device. No analytics data leaves your device — all usage statistics are stored locally.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication — anonymous sign-in only. Firebase Privacy
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app purchases. RevenueCat Privacy
  • Google Play Billing — processes payments. Google Privacy

4. Data Storage

Lab progress, streaks, XP, and usage statistics are stored on your device only (localStorage and Capacitor Preferences). We do not operate any backend server that stores your data.

5. Children's Privacy

TechForge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact
